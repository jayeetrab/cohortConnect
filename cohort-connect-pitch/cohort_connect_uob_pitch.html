COHORT CONNECT

A placement intelligence platform that turns CVs, alumni networks, employer demand, and live cohort analytics into faster student outcomes.

Pitch deck for University of Bristol adoption

The problem

Placements are still managed like admin, not like a talent engine

Students miss warm access

Students depend on cold applications and fragmented job boards, while alumni who could help are hard to identify and activate at scale.

Employers search with weak signals

Keyword search misses strong candidates whose skills are described differently, creating false negatives and slower hiring.

Placement teams work manually

Career services still rely on email chains, static PDFs, and spreadsheet tracking, limiting visibility, speed, and scalability.

Universities lose live insight

Without closed-loop tracking, programmes cannot see what skills drive offers, which channels convert, or where curriculum gaps sit.

The answer

One platform connecting students, alumni, employers, and faculty

📄

AI profile creation

Upload a CV once; the platform extracts skills, projects, experience, and builds a searchable profile automatically.

🧠

Semantic matching

Natural-language search maps employer needs to student capability, not just exact keywords on a PDF.

🤝

Alumni activation

The system identifies relevant alumni, drafts referral emails, and reduces the friction of giving back.

📈

Live dashboards

Placement teams track searches, referrals, interviews, offers, and skill demand in real time.

🕶️

Blind hiring mode

Names, gender, and photos can be hidden so employers review skill alignment first.

🧾

Auto-brochure output

Generate employer-ready cohort brochures and outreach assets without manual PowerPoint rebuilding.

User journey

How the closed loop works

1. Student uploads CV

The profile agent extracts skills, projects, experience, and embeddings for semantic understanding.

2. Employer searches naturally

A recruiter describes the ideal candidate in plain language rather than forcing keyword filters.

3. Platform ranks best matches

Candidates are scored by semantic fit, with reasons, strengths, and gaps surfaced instantly.

4. Alumni referrals are drafted

If a relevant alumnus exists, the platform generates a warm referral email for approval in seconds.

5. University learns from outcomes

Searches, interviews, offers, and channel performance feed dashboards and future curriculum insight.

Architecture

Built on a practical, modern AI stack

Frontend

Student, alumni, employer, and admin portals in React or Vue with responsive dashboards and upload flows.

Application layer

FastAPI powers CV upload, profile management, job matching, referral workflows, analytics, and authentication.

Agentic AI

LangChain and LangGraph orchestrate CV parsing, matching, referral drafting, and outcome analytics agents.

LLM layer

Gemini or GPT-class models support structured extraction, match explanations, and outreach generation.

Data layer

PostgreSQL with pgvector stores relational data and embeddings in one university-manageable stack.

Governance

Supports OAuth/JWT, consent-led data use, blind hiring, audit logs, and GDPR-aligned operation.
